Personal

My name is pronounced KELL-see GRIN-dee and I use the pronouns she/her/hers. (Fun fact: my last name, Grinde, is actually the name of the town in Norway where my great-grandfather grew up.) Except for five years in Seattle for graduate school, and lots of travel, I've spent the rest of my life in Minnesota and am very happy to call this state home. In my free time, I enjoy playing and watching soccer, biking, listening to The Current, and eating — but not cooking — good food.
